Smart Environment Management Nhume

Our smart environment management system is called Nhume ; which is

Shona name for “messenger”.It uses wireless sensor network to monitor the enviro nment within a local authority. Sensors are placed at strategic points such as sewer ponds, water treatment p lants, industrial areas and streets. These sensors detect the presence of harmful toxins in water and air. The data is relayed to a central repository on the cloud where it is analyzed using artificial intelligent models to come up with analytical interpretations which are displayed on a web dashboard . Abnormal levels of toxins are flagged.

Smart Bus Entry Control

This system is an automated boom gate which is controlled by software.

It monitors the entry and exit of passenger vehicles (buses and kombies) into and out of council owned bus terminus. The system eliminates human intervention in logging in and logging out vehicles in bus terminus. It uses RFID cards to log clients in and out of the terminus. Each client is given a unique account number. Transactions can be monitored real time. Revenue collected is automatically computed. This system reduces corruption at bus ranks.
